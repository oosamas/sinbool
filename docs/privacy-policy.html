Sinbool Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Summary: Sinbool is designed for children and is fully COPPA compliant. We do not collect personal information from children. The app works entirely offline with optional cloud sync for progress.

1. Introduction

Sinbool ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our Islamic stories application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Sinbool.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Names or personal identifiers
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Photos or videos
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Any personally identifiable information from children

2.2 Information We May Collect

  • Anonymous Usage Data: Non-personal analytics about app usage (which stories are popular, app crashes) to improve the app
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes
  • Progress Data: Reading progress and bookmarks stored locally on your device or anonymously in the cloud

3. COPPA Compliance

Sinbool complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We:

  • Do not collect personal information from children under 13
  • Do not require registration or login
  • Do not display behavioral advertising
  • Do not share data with third parties for marketing
  • Provide parental controls for content and usage

4. Data Storage

All user data (progress, bookmarks, settings) is stored:

  • Locally: On your device using secure storage
  • Cloud (Optional): If you enable sync, data is stored anonymously on Firebase servers with encryption

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports to fix bugs
  • Firebase Firestore: Optional cloud storage for progress

These services are configured to not collect personal information and comply with children's privacy requirements.

6. Parental Controls

Sinbool includes parental controls that allow parents to:

  • Set a PIN to restrict access to settings
  • Set daily time limits for app usage
  • Control which content categories are accessible
  • Clear all local data

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect data:

  • Encrypted local storage on device
  • Secure HTTPS connections for any network requests
  • No sensitive data transmitted or stored

8. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Cloud data (if enabled) can be deleted through the app settings or by contacting us.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Delete your data through app settings
  • Disable cloud sync at any time
  • Contact us with questions or concerns

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@sinbool.com
Website: https://sinbool.com

For Parents: If you believe your child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.
